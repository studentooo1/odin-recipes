Ugali

Description

Ugali is one of the most popular dishes in Africa so much so that it has led to be nick named "African cake" maily because of its resemblance in structure, but make no mistake the taste is far from that of cakes regardless its an in-demand quisine by the locals. It's a maize flour based dish that can be eaten with most stews but stews containing fish, chicken, beef or just meat in general is preferred. .

Ingredients

  • A pot
  • Maize flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add two cups of water to the pot and heat to boil.
  2. Add a cup of maize flour.
  3. Stir the mixture with a wooden spatula until the mixture becomes solid.
  4. Turn the pot upside down on a plate and gently lift it to get the desired shape.
